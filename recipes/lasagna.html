Lasagna

Description

his is a classic lasagna recipe featuring layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It's a comforting and satisfying dish perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (15 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Cheese Mixture:

  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Other:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add ground beef or sausage and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon. Add onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer, cover, and cook for at least 30 minutes, or up to 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  2. Combine Cheese Mixture and Cook Noodles: In a medium bowl, combine ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, egg, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions until al dente. Drain and rinse with cold water to prevent sticking.
  3. Assemble and Bake: Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Top with 3 lasagna noodles, overlapping if necessary. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles, then top with half of the remaining meat sauce and 1 cup of mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers: 3 noodles, remaining ricotta mixture, remaining meat sauce, and remaining mozzarella cheese. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the lasagna is heated through. Let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
