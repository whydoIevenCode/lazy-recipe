Chocolate Cheesecake Recipe

Description

This rich and decadent chocolate cheesecake is a perfect dessert for any occasion. It features a creamy chocolate filling on a chocolate cookie crust, topped with a smooth chocolate ganache.

Ingredients

Crust:

  • 1 1/2 cups chocolate cookie crumbs (e.g., Oreo crumbs)
  • 5 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Filling:

  • 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream

Ganache:

  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 4 ounces semi-sweet chocolate, finely chopped

Steps

  1. Prepare the Crust: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). In a medium bowl, combine cookie crumbs and melted butter. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan. Bake for 10 minutes. Let cool completely.
  2. Make the Filling: In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar with an electric mixer until smooth and creamy. Beat in cocoa powder and vanilla extract. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Gradually beat in heavy cream.
  3. Bake the Cheesecake: Pour the filling over the cooled crust. Bake for 55-65 minutes, or until the center is almost set but still has a slight jiggle. Turn off the oven, crack the oven door slightly, and let the cheesecake cool in the oven for 1 hour. Then, remove from the oven and chill in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight.
  4. Prepare the Ganache: Heat heavy cream in a small saucepan over medium heat until simmering. Remove from heat and add chopped chocolate. Let stand for 1 minute, then whisk until smooth.
  5. Assemble and Serve: Pour the ganache over the chilled cheesecake. Spread evenly. Chill for another 30 minutes to allow the ganache to set. Run a thin knife around the edge of the pan to release the sides. Remove the sides of the springform pan. Slice and serve.
